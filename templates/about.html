This blog is maintained by Oz Nahum Tiram, a passionate Python programmer, and FOSS evangelist, who really likes Debian.

Currently he works for Science+Computing AG where he supports Linux users designing German Autos and doing CFD using HPC clusters. Besides installing and configuring Linux workstations and clusters, he writes scripts, provides 2nd level support and does all kind of other things, like staring at the blinking lights in the server room. When he is not doing computer related stuff, he likes cycling and rock climbing.
